We are a group of animal advocacy committed to promoting awareness and education of animal welfare. We work with local communities with the aim of ensuring the animals are free from cruelty.

For companion animals such as dogs and cats, we subscribe to the principles of rescue, rehabilitation, neuter, adoption and release. As for wild animals we subscribe to the principle of rescue, rehabilitation and release.
