About Us

Our Role

We regularly engage with government agencies, local authorities, NGO's and individuals in discussing matters pertaining to animal welfare particularly on how the Animal Welfare Act 2015 can be meaningfully enforced and effectively implemented.

Our Objectives

We aim for a higher level of awareness amongst the people of Malaysia on the Animal Act 2015 and the Wildlife Conservation Act 2010, by effectively dissemination information on these acts through our programs.

We also aim to break the chain of cruelty towards animals through various initiatives and programs where we look to actively engage community involvement. Please support us in our journey to protect animals from cruelty.
